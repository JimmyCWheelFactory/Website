About

Jimmy C. Wheel Factory Inc. was founded in 2019 and is owned by Jimmy C. Hildman and Gabriela Schmidov.  

In 1985, Jimmy started his career as a Tier One Automotive OE Wheel Supplier with Hayes Lemmerz International.  Hayes manufactured and supplied brand new aluminum wheels to companies such as General Motors, Ford, Chrysler, and Isuzu. While working in the OE industry, Jimmy gained knowledge of the aluminum wheel process in its entirety. In 1998, Jimmy moved into the refinishing/aftermarket wheel industry with Transwheel Corporation. From the foundry to the final stage, the box to the assembly plant, Jimmy now brings to New York decades of aluminum wheel expertise. In late 2019, Jimmy C. Wheel Factory Inc. now brings our craft to the Long Island NY area where we are honored to serve our customers/partners with their aluminum wheel refinish and replacements needs.
