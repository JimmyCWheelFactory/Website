Services

Straightening

Bent rim? We can inspect your wheels to find if we can repair your wheels so you don’t have to spend on a brand new wheel.
Welding

You don’t always need a brand new wheel. For cracks that come with driving those rough roads, we can weld and seal wheels that are otherwise structurally sound.
Polishing

Bring us your old and worn wheels. Not only do we make them shine like new, we’ll make your ride safer too.
CNC Machining

Part of our production is our state of the art CNC machining process. This allows us to ensure ultra-precise production that’s always consistent.
Paint & Powder Coating

Depending on your needs we offer both paint and powder coating. Customize your wheels to get the sleek or flashy look you want.
TPMS Replacement

Tire light always on? When you choose to restore with us, let us know and we can change your TPMS complimentary.
Free Mount & Balance

Maintaining proper balance is crucial to the comfortability and the safety of how a vehicle drives. We ensure your wheels have been installed with precision.
B2B Pick Up and Delivery

We offer both free pick up and delivery for the auto shops we work with. You can schedule a pick up by calling or by filling out a pick up form.
Wheel Locating

If you have a specific type of style or brand you’re looking for we will not only help spruce it up, we also use our network of suppliers to make sure you get the perfect look.
Retail Center

Stop by and take a look at what we have in stock at our retail center. Our representatives can answer any questions.
